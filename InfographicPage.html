Food For Those in Need?

Map Details

This infographic tells the story of FoodShare and poverty in Wisconsin in 2013. Trends in FoodShare benefits received and need (defined as below the poverty line) are seen on the map. Additionally, the stories of Milwaukee and Menominee Counties are given in more detail beause they are the two counties in the state with the highest percent of people below the poverty line.

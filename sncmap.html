St. Norbert College Campus Map

Map Details

This map is a campus map of St. Norbert College, located in De Pere, Wisconsin. It was created using an antique style, as if it was from the late 1800s, when the school was founded. Designing was completed in Adobe Illustrator, with the embossed look hand drawn with a stylus. The paper-like background was created in Adobe Photoshop.

Software, Techniques, and Resources Utilized

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Hurricane Katrina Rainfall

Map Details

The was the first interactive map I ever made, which also meant this was my first experience with JavaScript! I created this map using Leaflet.js. This map shows Hurricane Katrina rainfall amounts in 3 hour increments. The user can sequence through time to see the track of the storm on the day it made landfall. Scroll down to see a video demo of the map, or click on the image above to open the map in a new tab.

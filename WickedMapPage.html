Defy Gravity!

Map Details

The purpose of this map was to take an existing style and create a map using that style. This map is in the style of the Broadway musical 'Wicked'. The points of interest are the locations of the 2015 United States tour, along with some on-going worldwide production locations.

Software, Techniques, and Resources Utilized

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • CartoCSS
  • Mapbox Studio
